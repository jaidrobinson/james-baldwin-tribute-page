James Baldwin: A Tribute Page

James Baldwin in Holland in 1974. Dutch National Archives / Wikimedia Commons

James Baldwin (1924-1987) was a highly acclaimed, highly influential writer and civil rights activist. His writing explored the complexity of topics like race, class, and sexuality. Baldwin's work continues to influence Black and queer art around the world.
